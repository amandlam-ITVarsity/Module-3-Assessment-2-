About our dessert

Malva pudding is a sweet pudding of South African origin. It contains apricot jam and has a spongy caramelised texture. A cream sauce is always poured over it while it is hot, and it is usually served warm with custard and/or ice-cream. Many South African restaurants offer it.

Fluffy, creamy and sweet!
